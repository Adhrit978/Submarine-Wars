Submarine Wars

Hi! Thanks for coming to look at my game. Here are a few things you need to know before playing:-

Note-My game is only for laptops/desktops, so if you're on a phone/tablet it won't work :(

  1. Characters:-

    • Player (Everything on the left half of the screen):-

      • Submarine-Use arrow keys to move it
      • Launcher-Launches missiles and homing missiles
      • Missile-Press the space bar to launch
      • Homing Missile-Automatically finds the computer’s submarine, instead of flying in a straight line like a missile-Press the s key to launch
      • Number of Missiles Left-65 at the start
      • Number of Homing Missiles Left-10 at the start
      • Life Bar-Shows how much life the player has left (200 at the start). Goes down by 10 when the player’s submarine is hit by a missile. If it becomes 0, the player loses.

    • Computer (Everything on the right half of the screen):-

      • Submarine-Moved by AI
      • Launcher-Launches missiles and homing missiles
      • Missile-Launched by AI
      • Homing Missile-Automatically finds the player’s submarine, instead of flying in a straight line like a missile-Launched by AI
      • Number of Missiles Left-85 at the start
      • Number of Homing Missiles Left-15 at the start
      • Life Bar-Shows how much life the computer has left (300 at the start). Goes down by 10 when the computer’s submarine is hit by a missile. If it becomes 0, the computer loses.
    • Obstacles-Absorb all the player’s missiles and homing missiles, and about half of the computer’s missiles. The computer’s homing missiles are unaffected.
    • Plus Life-Increases the player’s life by 20 when hit by one of his/her missiles/homing missiles
    • Plus Homing Missile-Increases the number of homing missiles the player has by 1 when hit by a missile/homing missiles
    • Background

  2. Rules:-

    • Objective: The player aims to reduce the computer’s life to 0 while keeping his/her own positive. The computer aims to reduce the player’s life to 0 while keeping its own positive
    • The computer has more of everything:-
      • 85 missiles, 20 more than the player
      • 15 homing missiles, 5 more than the player
      • 300 life, 100 more than the player
      • This is because it makes the game much more challenging; if it was easy, everyone would do it.
